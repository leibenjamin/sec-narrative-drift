FORM 10-K

ITEM 1. BUSINESS

We design, manufacture, and market a portfolio of consumer devices and services across multiple regions and sales channels.

ITEM 1A. RISK FACTORS

Our business depends on a complex global supply chain that relies on third-party manufacturing partners and logistics providers. Disruptions from geopolitical events, labor constraints, or transportation delays can reduce product availability, increase costs, and impact our ability to meet demand. These risk factors can pressure margins and delay product launches across multiple regions.

Demand for our devices and services can shift quickly due to competitive offerings, pricing pressure, or changes in customer preferences. If we do not anticipate these changes, inventory levels may rise and promotional activity could increase. Risk factors also include regulatory scrutiny and changes in privacy expectations that may require product or platform adjustments.

We rely on third-party components and software to deliver key features, and security vulnerabilities in those components could harm our products and services. Cybersecurity incidents, including data breaches or service outages, could result in reputational harm, litigation, and significant remediation costs. These risk factors may also affect the trust of customers and partners.
